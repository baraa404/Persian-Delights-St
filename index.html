Featured Recipes

Ghormeh Sabzi (Herb Stew)

The crown jewel of Persian cuisine. A slow-cooked stew made with fresh herbs, kidney beans, and dried limes.

Zereshk Polo (Barberry Rice)

Fragrant saffron rice topped with tart barberries, usually served with perfectly roasted chicken.

Tahdig (Crispy Rice)

The golden, crunchy layer at the bottom of the rice pot. The most coveted part of any Persian meal.

Kitchen Tools

Essential for authentic cooking:

  • Non-stick Pot: Critical for achieving the perfect Tahdig.
  • Saffron Grinder: A small mortar and pestle for pulverizing saffron threads.
  • Skimmer Spoon: Perfect for fluffing and serving long-grain basmati rice.
